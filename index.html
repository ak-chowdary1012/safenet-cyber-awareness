What's out there

Seven threats shaping today's internet

Every category below is covered in depth in the Threat Library — what it looks like, why it works, and how to shut it down before it costs you anything.

High frequency

Phishing Attacks

Fake emails, texts and websites that impersonate trusted brands to steal your credentials.
Financial

UPI & Banking Frauds

QR code scams, fake "refund" requests and remote-access apps that drain bank accounts in seconds.
Long-term risk

Identity Theft

Stolen Aadhaar, PAN and personal details used to open accounts or commit fraud in your name.
Psychological

Social Engineering

Manipulation tactics — urgency, fear and authority — used to trick you into acting without thinking.
Targets students

Fake Job & Internship Scams

"Pay to get hired" offers and task-based scams that exploit job seekers and freshers.
Online safety

Cyberbullying & Harassment

Online abuse, stalking and image-based harassment — and the legal protections available to you.
Technical

Malware & Ransomware

Malicious software that spies on, locks, or destroys your files — and how infections actually start.

How SafeNet helps

From confusion to confidence, in three steps

STEP 01

Understand the threat

Browse plain-language breakdowns of every major scam type, with real examples of how messages, calls and websites actually look.

STEP 02

Train your instincts

Take interactive quizzes and play spot-the-scam mini-games until red flags become second nature.

STEP 03

Act fast if it happens

Know the exact helpline numbers, websites and steps to report a cyber crime — before more damage is done.

Been scammed? Act within minutes.

Call the National Cyber Crime Helpline — 1930

Or report online at cybercrime.gov.in. The first hour after a fraud is the most important — banks can often freeze a transaction if reported quickly.
