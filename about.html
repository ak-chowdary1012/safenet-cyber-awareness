The project

SafeNet: Digital Security Awareness and Cyber Fraud Prevention Program

India's rapid digital growth — UPI payments, online banking, e-commerce, and social media — has brought enormous convenience, but also a sharp rise in cyber crime. SafeNet exists to close the awareness gap, giving students, families, and first-time internet users the knowledge they need to navigate the digital world safely.

This website is the centrepiece of a broader outreach program that includes in-person awareness sessions, printed materials, and community engagement — all under the mentorship of Smt. Anjana.

Project pillar

Education, Digital Literacy & Community Empowerment

Education

Plain-language resources that turn confusing technical threats into understandable, actionable knowledge.

Digital Literacy

Practical skills — recognising phishing, securing accounts, and verifying information — for everyday digital life.

Community Empowerment

Reaching schools, colleges, and underserved communities through outreach sessions and a sustainable online platform.
